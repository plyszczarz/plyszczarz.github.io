Bin picking problem solution on Doosan (Python)





This project tackles the challenge of efficiently selecting randomly positioned and oriented elements from containers, planes, or other components, such as those found on a production line, as part of the Bin Picking process. The goal of the project was to use the technology of vision systems and a cobot robot from Doosan and Universal Robots in order to optimally solve and perform the mentioned task. Python was used to integrate the devices and the algorithm itself
