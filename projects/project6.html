Drones control in a 3D environment with obstacles (C++)





Project of program showcasing the dynamic interaction of two drones within a scene, granting user the freedom to pilot them in any direction and distance. Furthermore, user has the capability to introduce and remove obstacles into or from the scene. Notably, the code ensures that drones are prohibited from colliding with or landing on obstacles, instead employing avoidance strategies and safely landing in alternative locations. The visualization of simulation is presented in GNUplot
