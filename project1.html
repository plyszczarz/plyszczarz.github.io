QT app visualizing Hydro-station sensors data (C++)





Project of Qt application dedicated to the monitoring of environmental sensor data. The application interfaces with a variety of sensors including ultrasonic distance, humidity, water flow speed, and DHT11 temperature sensors. Its primary purpose is to provide real-time visualization of sensor readings and to alert users to any critical conditions. The application is designed with a user-friendly interface, enabling easy interpretation of sensor data. It offers seamless integration with multiple sensors, ensuring comprehensive monitoring of environmental parameters. Additionally, it includes an alert system to notify users of any readings that exceed predetermined thresholds.
