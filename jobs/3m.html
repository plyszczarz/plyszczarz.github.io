About 3M

3M Poland is a branch of the global science-based company 3M, known for its innovation and diverse portfolio of products across various industries, including healthcare, automotive, electronics, safety, and consumer goods. Operating in Poland, 3M focuses on delivering advanced solutions that improve daily life and drive business efficiency. The company combines state-of-the-art materials, technology, and expertise to create products such as adhesives, abrasives, personal protective equipment, and healthcare solutions.
