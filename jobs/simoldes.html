About Simoldes Plasticos

Simoldes Plasticos is a leading provider of high-quality plastic injection molded parts for the automotive industry. The company is known for its commitment to innovation, precision, and sustainable manufacturing processes. With state-of-the-art facilities and a highly skilled workforce, Simoldes Plasticos plays a critical role in shaping the future of automotive component manufacturing, delivering products that meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

My Role at Simoldes

As an Automation Engineer at Simoldes, my primary responsibilities include designing, programming, and maintaining robotic systems used in the manufacturing process. I work with a team of engineers to develop efficient automation solutions that improve production speed and ensure consistent product quality. My role involves integrating advanced robotic systems, troubleshooting issues, and optimizing workflows to reduce downtime and increase productivity. I play a key part in implementing innovative solutions that enhance the company's competitive edge in the automotive market.








Technologies I Use

In my role at Simoldes, I utilize a variety of technologies and tools to ensure smooth operation and optimization of the manufacturing process. Key technologies include:

  • PLC Programming: Used to control machinery and automate processes within the production line.
  • Robotics: Integration and maintenance of robotic systems for precise and efficient manufacturing.
  • Industrial Networks: Use of industrial Ethernet and fieldbus networks to ensure reliable communication between devices.
