My Role at Nokia

As a C++ Software Engineer at Nokia, my responsibilities include designing and implementing software solutions for 5G technologies, specifically within the Layer 2 (L2) Packet Scheduling (PS) layer. My role involves creating and refining software enhancements to improve system performance and reliability. I tackle complex engineering challenges and work on integrating advanced C++ techniques to drive innovation in our 5G solutions. This includes optimizing scheduling and data exchange processes, ensuring efficient handling of network resources, and contributing to the overall advancement of our cutting-edge radio technologies. My work is integral to advancing the capabilities of our 5G networks and maintaining Nokia's position at the forefront of telecommunications technology.
