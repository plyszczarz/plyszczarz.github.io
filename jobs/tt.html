About Transition Technologies

My Role at TTCS

As a Robotics Engineer at Transition Technologies, I worked on a bin picking project utilizing a Doosan collaborative robot programmed with Python. My responsibilities included designing, programming, and optimizing robotic solutions to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of the bin picking process. I was responsible for selecting the appropriate vision system and end effectors to optimize the project, ensuring precise object detection and manipulation. By integrating these components with smart controls, I enabled the cobot to perform reliable tasks, ultimately streamlining operations and improving overall productivity.








Project Results

I successfully completed the project with a high success rate in robot bin picking accuracy, demonstrating the system’s effectiveness. Following the project, I further developed and refined the concept using a different robot and vision system as part of my personal development. This enhanced version of the system was later utilized as a key component of my thesis work.
