Autonomous mapping robot ROS (Python)





Computer simulation project of a mapping robot in ROS 2. After being placed in the selected map, the robot moves and collects information from the LIDAR distance sensor. This data enables it to understand the layout of its environment and construct a detailed map. Tools such as Gazebo and Rviz were used to visualize and create realistic models of environments as well as to simulate robot behavior. The algorithm used is SLAM (Simultaneous Loaclization and Mapping), which enables accurate mapping of the surroundings while the robot is moving and the Nav2 navigation environment.
